About Us

Founded in 2023, MEENAKSHI MUKUNDHA TIMES (MM Times) is an R&D-driven company dedicated to building innovative, sustainable, and affordable technologies in energy, refrigeration, air conditioning, and motor systems. Backed by SISFS and NIDHI PRAYAS grants, we have published two patents and are actively developing next-gen products for households, industries, and communities. Our mission is to deliver eco-friendly, efficient, and durable solutions while offering multi-service support that meets customer needs and drives national progress.

Vision

"Anyone who strives, deserves comfort."

Mission

Our mission is to design and deliver eco-friendly, affordable, and durable appliances for households and communities. Through strong research, innovation, and customer-focused services, we provide efficient, low-maintenance solutions that meet diverse needs while promoting sustainability, national development, and a greener, better future for all.
