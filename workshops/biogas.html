Biogas & Biomass Energy Production Workshop

About the Biogas & Biomass Energy Production Workshop

The Biogas & Biomass Energy Production Workshop is an immersive, practical training designed to teach participants how organic waste can be converted into clean, renewable energy. Through hands-on sessions, learners explore anaerobic digestion, biogas plant mechanisms, biomass processing, and sustainable energy engineering. This workshop is ideal for environmental students, sustainability enthusiasts, innovators, farm-level practitioners, and beginners eager to understand waste-to-energy systems. By the end of the workshop, every participant will create and test a fully functional small-scale biogas unit.
