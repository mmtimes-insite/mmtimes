Natural Soap & Biodegradable Detergent Making Workshop

About the Natural Soap & Biodegradable Detergent Workshop

The Natural Soap & Biodegradable Detergent Making Workshop is a hands-on, practical training designed to teach participants how eco-friendly soaps and detergents are formulated, blended, molded, cured, and packaged. This workshop is ideal for students, hobbyists, entrepreneurs, and sustainability enthusiasts who want to learn natural product formulation. By the end of the workshop, every participant will create multiple soap bars and biodegradable household detergents using safe, natural ingredients.
