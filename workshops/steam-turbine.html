Steam Turbine Workshop

About the Steam Turbine Workshop

The Steam Turbine Workshop is a hands-on program designed to teach participants how steam energy is converted into high-speed rotational power. This workshop introduces learners to steam thermodynamics, turbine blade mechanisms, nozzle design, shaft rotation, and practical power conversion.

By the end of the workshop, participants will build, assemble, and run a functional small-scale steam turbine capable of demonstrating real energy conversion ‚Äî sometimes even powering an LED.

üîß Turbine Assembly & Blade Setup

üß™ Steam Flow Testing & Calibration

‚ö° Power Output Demonstration
