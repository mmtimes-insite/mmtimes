About the IC Engines Assembly Workshop

The IC Engines Assembly Workshop is a fully practical, hands-on training designed to teach participants how internal combustion engines are assembled, disassembled, inspected, and diagnosed. This workshop is ideal for mechanical engineering students, automotive enthusiasts, bikers, hobby mechanics, and beginners curious about engine mechanics. By the end of the program, every participant will assemble and troubleshoot real 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines while learning the complete working cycle and maintenance procedures.
