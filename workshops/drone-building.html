Drone Building Workshop

About the Drone Building Workshop

The Drone Building & Programming Workshop is a practical, hands-on training designed to teach participants how drones are engineered, assembled, calibrated, and programmed for real-time applications. This workshop is ideal for engineering students, hobbyists, tech clubs, innovators, and beginners who want to explore UAV technology. By the end of the workshop, every participant will build, configure, and test a fully functional drone capable of stable flight and programmable behaviors.

üîß Hands-On Drone Assembly

Build a drone from scratch: frame setup, motor installation, propeller balancing, ESC wiring, power distribution, radio receiver integration, and flight controller connections. Every step is guided professionally for a perfect and safe drone build.

üß™ System Calibration & Testing

Learn ESC calibration, motor direction testing, gyro & accelerometer calibration, radio binding, failsafe setup, sensor tuning, and PID tuning basics. All modules are tested until the drone achieves stable and predictable performance.

Practice takeoff, hovering, movement, landing, and controlled directional flying. Learn safety protocols, obstacle-free movement, manual mode, altitude hold, and semi-autonomous operations with real-time troubleshooting.
