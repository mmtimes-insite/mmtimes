Solar Water Heater Workshop

About the Solar Water Heater Workshop

The Solar Water Heater Workshop is a hands-on training program designed to teach participants how solar thermal systems capture, store, and utilize heat energy to warm water efficiently. This workshop is ideal for engineering students, sustainability enthusiasts, hobbyists, innovators, and anyone interested in renewable energy technologies. By the end of the workshop, participants will design, construct, and test a fully functional solar water heating system capable of effective heat absorption and thermal storage.
