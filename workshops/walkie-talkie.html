Walkie-Talkie System Workshop

About the Walkie-Talkie System Workshop

The Walkie-Talkie System Workshop is a hands-on practical training focused on designing and building RF-based communication systems. Participants will learn the fundamentals of RF circuits, antenna design, signal modulation, and audio transmission. This workshop is ideal for electronics students, hobbyists, tech clubs, and innovators who want to understand wireless communication technology. By the end of the workshop, every participant will design, assemble, and test a fully functional walkie-talkie capable of short-range wireless communication.
