Water Filtration & Purification Workshop

About the Water Filtration & Purification Workshop

The Water Filtration & Purification Workshop is a hands-on, practical training designed to teach participants how modern filtration systems work, how contaminants are removed, and how clean, safe drinking water is produced. This workshop is ideal for students, environmental learners, innovators, science clubs, and anyone interested in water technology. By the end of the workshop, every participant will design, assemble, and test a fully functional multi-stage water filtration unit.
