Autonomous Vehicle Workshop

About the Autonomous Vehicle Workshop

The Autonomous Vehicle Workshop is a hands-on training program focused on building, programming, and testing miniature self-driving cars. Participants learn computer vision, path planning, obstacle detection, and sensor-based navigation systems. This workshop is ideal for engineering students, robotics enthusiasts, innovators, and beginners looking to explore autonomous mobility. By the end of the program, every participant will develop a functional prototype autonomous vehicle capable of real-time decision-making and automated movement.
