FPV (First Person View) Video Transmission Workshop

About the FPV (First Person View) Video Transmission Workshop

The FPV (First Person View) Video Transmission Workshop is a fully practical, hands-on training designed to teach participants how to build, configure, and test real-time wireless video systems. This workshop is ideal for robotics students, tech hobbyists, drone builders, innovators, and beginners exploring live video communication. By the end of the workshop, every participant will create a working FPV system capable of transmitting live video for robotics, surveillance, or drone applications.
