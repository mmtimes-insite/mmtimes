IoT (Internet of Things) Workshop

About the IoT (Internet of Things) Workshop

The IoT (Internet of Things) Workshop is a fully practical, hands-on training program that introduces participants to the world of connected smart devices. Through guided sessions, learners will understand how sensors, microcontrollers, and communication protocols work together to collect data and control devices remotely over the internet. This workshop is ideal for students, hobbyists, innovators, tech clubs, and beginners interested in modern automation and smart technology. By the end of the program, every participant will design, program, and deploy real IoT projects such as smart lighting systems, environmental monitoring, and cloud-connected dashboards.
