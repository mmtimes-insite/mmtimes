Vertical Farming & Hydroponics Workshop

About the Hydroponics Workshop

The Vertical Farming & Hydroponics Workshop is an immersive, hands-on program that teaches participants how modern soil-less farming systems are designed, assembled, and optimized for continuous food production. This workshop is ideal for biology students, green-tech enthusiasts, innovators, eco-clubs, and beginners who want to learn sustainable farming methods. By the end of the workshop, every participant will build and operate a fully functional hydroponic farming system.

üîß Hands-On Hydroponic System Building
