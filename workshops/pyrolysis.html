Plastic-to-Fuel Pyrolysis Workshop

About the Plastic-to-Fuel Pyrolysis Workshop

The Plastic-to-Fuel Pyrolysis Workshop is an intensive, hands-on training designed to teach participants how plastic waste is thermally decomposed into usable fuel through the process of pyrolysis. This workshop is ideal for engineering students, environmental innovators, sustainability enthusiasts, makers, and beginners interested in clean-energy conversion. By the end of the workshop, every participant will build, operate, and test a fully functional small-scale pyrolysis reactor.
