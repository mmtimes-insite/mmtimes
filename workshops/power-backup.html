Power Backup & Inverter Workshop

About the Power Backup & Inverter Workshop

The Power Backup & Inverter Workshop is a hands-on practical training designed to teach participants how mini UPS systems, battery backups, and inverter circuits are built, tested, and optimized for real-time usage. This workshop is ideal for engineering students, electronics hobbyists, makers, and beginners who want to understand AC/DC conversion, power handling, rectifiers, and inverter design. By the end of the program, every participant will assemble and test a working backup power system capable of running small appliances safely.
