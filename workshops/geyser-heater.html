Geyser & Water Heater Assembly Workshop

About the Geyser & Water Heater Assembly Workshop

The Geyser & Water Heater Assembly Workshop is a hands-on technical training where participants learn how heating systems function, how thermostats regulate temperature, and how electrical safety components like MCBs and wiring protections are implemented. This workshop is ideal for engineering students, home appliance enthusiasts, electricians-in-training, and beginners wanting practical experience. By the end of the workshop, participants assemble and test a fully working mini water heating system.
