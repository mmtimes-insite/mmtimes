Solar-Powered Oven Workshop

About the Solar-Powered Oven Workshop

The Solar-Powered Oven Workshop is a fully practical, hands-on learning program focused on teaching participants how solar energy can be captured, concentrated, and transformed into usable cooking heat. This workshop is ideal for students, sustainability enthusiasts, DIY hobbyists, innovators, and beginners interested in renewable energy systems. By the end of the workshop, every participant will design, assemble, and test a functioning solar oven capable of cooking food using only sunlight.
