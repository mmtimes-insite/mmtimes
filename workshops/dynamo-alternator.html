Dynamo & Alternator Workshop

About the Dynamo & Alternator Workshop

The Dynamo & Alternator Workshop is a hands-on training program focused on understanding how mechanical motion is converted into electrical energy. Participants learn the principles of electromagnetism, rotating machines, coil winding, magnetic flux, and current generation. This workshop is perfect for engineering students, hobbyists, science clubs, and beginners curious about how real power-generation systems work. By the end of the workshop, every participant will assemble, test, and generate usable electricity using their own working dynamo or alternator model.

In this workshop, participants will learn the complete working principles of both AC Generators (Alternators) and DC Generators (Dynamos), including electromagnetic induction, armature operation, magnetic field interaction, commutators, rectification, and voltage generation mechanisms.

However, for the practical assembly & testing module, the workshop must select any ONE generator type from the options below:

This ensures every learner completes a full hands-on generator build while gaining strong conceptual understanding of both AC and DC electricity generation systems.
