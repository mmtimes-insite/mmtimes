Student Voice Assistant Robot Workshop

About the Student Voice Assistant Robot Workshop

The Student Voice Assistant Robot Workshop is a practical, hands-on training where participants learn to build a personalized robot that responds using student-recorded voices. This workshop is ideal for beginners, tech enthusiasts, schools, hobbyists, and innovators who want to explore speech recognition, API-based automation, and robotic interaction. By the end of the workshop, every participant will build and configure a fully functional voice-controlled robot capable of customized responses and smart assistive behavior.
